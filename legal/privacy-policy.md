# Privacy Policy — Gajju

**Last Updated:** March 2, 2026  
**Effective Date:** March 2, 2026

## 1) Scope
This Privacy Policy explains how Gajju ("we", "our", "us") handles personal information when you use the Gajju mobile app.

Gajju is designed as a local-first app. Most personal content remains on your device unless you choose to use features that rely on third-party services.

## 2) Information We Process

### A) Data stored on your device
The app stores most data locally on your phone/tablet, including:
- Profile name and mascot name
- Journal/reflection entries
- Reading progress, streaks, and quest state
- App settings and preferences
- Optional profile photo reference
- Prayer Lock preferences/status (for supported iOS devices)

We do not currently operate our own backend database for this local app content.

### B) Data sent to third parties when you use specific features
1. AI Chat (xAI Grok API):
   - If you use Gita chat, your prompt and recent conversation context are sent to xAI's Grok API to generate a response.
2. Subscriptions (RevenueCat + Apple App Store / Google Play):
   - Subscription events, entitlement status, and management metadata are processed to enable purchases and restore flows.
   - Payment card data is processed by Apple/Google, not by us.
3. Notifications (optional):
   - If you enable notifications, push-related identifiers/tokens may be processed by Apple Push Notification service (APNs), Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM), and Expo notification infrastructure to deliver reminders.
4. Support requests:
   - If you contact support, we process the information you provide (for example, email content and related troubleshooting details).

## 3) Why We Process Information
We process information to:
- Provide and operate app features you use
- Deliver optional reminders/notifications you request
- Process and restore subscription entitlements
- Respond to support requests
- Maintain app reliability and prevent abuse

## 4) Legal Bases (EEA/UK Users)
Where applicable, we rely on:
- Contract: to provide requested app features/subscriptions
- Consent: for optional permissions (for example, notifications, photo access)
- Legitimate interests: app security, abuse prevention, and service improvement
- Legal obligations: where required by law

## 5) Data Sharing and Third Parties
We do **not** sell your personal data and do **not** use in-app advertising trackers for targeted ads.

We share data only with service providers needed to run features you choose:
- xAI (Grok API): [xAI Privacy Policy](https://x.ai/legal/privacy-policy)
- RevenueCat: [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)
- Apple: [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy)
- Google Play: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- Expo: [Expo Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy)

## 6) International Data Transfers
Third-party providers may process data in countries outside your own. These providers are responsible for implementing appropriate transfer safeguards under their own legal frameworks.

## 7) Data Retention
- Local app data remains on your device until you delete it in-app or uninstall the app.
- Subscription and transaction records are retained by RevenueCat/Apple/Google under their own retention policies.
- Support communications are retained only as long as needed to resolve requests, comply with law, and maintain records.

## 8) Your Choices and Rights
You can:
- Clear local app data in app: `Settings -> Account -> Clear on-device data`
- Uninstall the app to remove local stored data
- Disable permissions (notifications/photo access) in device settings
- Manage/cancel subscriptions in Apple or Google account settings

Depending on your region, you may also have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, or data portability.  
To make a privacy request, email **gajju.help@gmail.com**.

## 9) Children's Privacy
Gajju is not intended for children under 13 (or higher minimum age required by local law). If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

## 10) Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

## 11) Policy Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date shows the latest revision.

## 12) Contact
Privacy questions and rights requests: **gajju.help@gmail.com**
