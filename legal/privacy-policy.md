# Privacy Policy — Gajju

**Last Updated:** February 28, 2026  
**Effective Date:** February 28, 2026

## 1) Overview
Gajju is a local-first Hindu spiritual companion app. This policy explains what data is used in the app, where it is processed, and your controls.

## 2) Local-First Model
Most app data is stored on your device (local storage), including:
- Profile name and mascot name
- Journal/reflection entries
- Reading/progress/streak state
- In-app settings
- Optional profile photo reference

We do not run our own backend that stores this personal app data.

## 3) Data Processed by Third Parties
Certain optional features require third-party services:

### A) AI Chat (Google Gemini API)
If you use the in-app Gita chat, your chat prompts and conversation context are sent from your device to Google's Gemini API to generate responses.

- Provider: Google
- Endpoint used by app: `https://generativelanguage.googleapis.com/...`
- Purpose: Generate AI chat responses
- Note: This data is processed by Google under Google's terms/policies.

### B) Subscriptions (RevenueCat + Apple/Google Stores)
If you purchase premium features, subscription status and purchase events are processed via RevenueCat and the platform stores (Apple App Store / Google Play).

- Purpose: Entitlement checks, restore purchases, subscription management
- Payment cards are processed by Apple/Google, not by us

## 4) Permissions Used
### Photo Library (Optional)
Used only when you choose to set/update a profile photo.

### Notifications (Optional)
Used for reminder notifications only when you grant permission.

We do not request camera or microphone recording permissions in the current app build.

## 5) No Account Requirement
You can use the app without creating an account. The app currently does not require Google/Apple sign-in for core usage.

## 6) Data Deletion and Control
Because app data is stored locally on your device:
- You can clear local app data from inside the app (Settings -> Account -> Clear on-device data)
- You can also uninstall the app to remove local app data

There is currently no server-side account profile to delete for local-only usage.

## 7) Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data.

We share data only as needed to provide features you use:
- Google (for AI chat requests you send)
- RevenueCat / Apple / Google Play (for subscription and purchase state)
- Expo services used by app runtime/tooling

## 8) Children's Privacy
Gajju is not intended for children under 13 (or higher age where required by local law). If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us.

## 9) Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. The "Last Updated" date reflects the latest revision.

## 10) Contact
For privacy questions, contact: **support@gajju.app**
